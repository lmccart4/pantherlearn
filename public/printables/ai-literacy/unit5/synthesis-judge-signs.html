Judge Benefit
Who benefits from this AI deployment?
(Name them. Specifically. Not “society.”)
Judge Cost
Who bears the cost or the risk when it fails?
(Who shows up on the bad side of the error bar?)
Judge Transparency
Can anyone see how it works? Who can’t?
(And who gets to decide what’s visible?)
Judge Contest
If it hurts you, can you challenge the outcome?
(If yes: how? If no: why not?)
