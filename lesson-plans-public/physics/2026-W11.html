Monday • Lesson 1
Battleship: Energy Quiz
March 9, 2026
Do Now
Students review the mission briefing for Operation Red Tide, a battleship-style game where physics knowledge serves as ammunition. They review the key concepts they'll be tested on: identifying energy types, recognizing transfers and transformations, defining systems, and applying conservation of energy reasoning.
Lesson Overview
0-2 min
Mission Briefing: Students read the rules and objectives for Operation Red Tide — correct answers let them fire on enemy ships, wrong answers mean a missed round
2-5 min
Review Objectives: Students review the four key areas being assessed — energy types, transfers, systems, and conservation of energy
5-35 min
Operation Red Tide: Students play the interactive Battleship: Energy Review game, answering energy questions to fire on enemy ships and sink the opposing fleet
35-40 min
Mission Debrief: Students reflect on which energy concepts gave them the most trouble and explain one question they got wrong using proper vocabulary
Exit Ticket
Students pick one question they answered incorrectly during the game and explain what the correct answer was and why, using vocabulary from earlier lessons (energy types, transfers, transformations, systems). They also preview the next topic: elastic potential energy and the EPE = ½kx² formula.
