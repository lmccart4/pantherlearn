Wednesday
Energy Skate Park Lab
March 25, 2026
Work & Energy
NGSS HS-PS3-1, HS-PS3-2 (Energy)
  • Observe how kinetic energy and gravitational potential energy trade off as a skater moves along a track
  • Use the live energy bar chart to connect simulation behavior to conservation of energy

Power: The Rate of Doing Work
March 26, 2026
Work & Energy
NGSS HS-PS3-1, HS-PS3-2 (Energy)
  • Define power as the rate at which work is done (P = W / t)
  • Calculate power in real-world scenarios using P = W / t and P = F × v
  • Compare power outputs of everyday machines, athletes, and animals
  • Explain why two systems can do the same work but have different power ratings
