Testing Experiment: Momentum Conservation
February 26, 2026
Momentum
NGSS HS-PS3-1, HS-PS3-2 (Energy)
  • Identify an isolated system for momentum in a marble-cart collision
  • Select initial and final states and sketch both states
  • Draw a momentum bar chart tracking momentum before and after collision
  • Use p = m × v to predict the cart velocity after collision
  • Compare predicted and measured values to evaluate conservation of momentum
  • Write a CER (Claim-Evidence-Reasoning) conclusion
Key Activities
  • Testing Experiment: Momentum Conservation
  • Materials & Method
  • Phase 1: Prediction
  • Task 4: Data & Prediction Calculation
  • Phase 2: Experiment
  • Phase 3: Analysis & Conclusion
Exit Ticket
Write your CER conclusion. Address whether momentum was conserved, cite your specific numerical evidence (predicted v_cart, measured v_cart), and explain your reasoning. Consider the assumptions and potential sources of error.
