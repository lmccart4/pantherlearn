Four Questions Courtroom

Every AI deployment hits the same four questions. Take a role, draw a scenario, push back where it doesn't hold.

Pick your roles

In your group of 4, each person owns one of the four judge questions for the entire round. You'll each answer your question for every scenario the group draws.

Judge Benefit

Who benefits from this?

If this AI works perfectly, who wins? Whose life gets easier or richer?
Judge Cost

Who bears the risk?

If it fails or misfires, who pays the price? Are they the same group as the winners?
Judge Transparency

Who can see how it works?

Can the people affected understand the AI's reasoning? Is the model open or hidden?
Judge Contest

If it hurts you, can you fight back?

Is there an appeal? A human in the loop? A way to challenge the decision?
