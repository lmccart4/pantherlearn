How to Use ▼
  1. Draw a card or browse through the deck using the arrows.
  2. Read the scenario on the front of the card.
  3. Tap the card to flip it and see different perspectives and discussion questions.
  4. Discuss with your group or class, then mark the card as discussed.
  5. Filter by category or difficulty to focus on specific topics.
  6. Switch views between single card and grid to see all dilemmas at once.
  7. Your progress is saved automatically — close and come back anytime.
