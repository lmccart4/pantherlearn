How to Use

  • Drag the charges on the canvas to change the distance between them
  • Use the sliders to adjust charge magnitudes and signs
  • Watch the force, direction, and proportional reasoning update in real time
  • The "What If" section shows how force would change with different values
  • Notice: force follows the inverse square law — same as gravity!
