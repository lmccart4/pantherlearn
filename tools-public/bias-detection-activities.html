Bias Detection Activities

Learn to identify and address bias in AI systems

How This Works ▼
  • Three activities teach you to spot, analyze, and fix bias in AI systems.
  • Activity 1 — Spot the Bias: Read AI scenarios and identify the type of bias at play. Then explain why the training data caused it.
  • Activity 2 — Who's Missing: Examine datasets and drag labels to identify which groups are underrepresented.
  • Activity 3 — Fix the Algorithm: Rank proposed solutions from most to least effective for each biased AI scenario.
  • 34 total points. Complete all three activities, then submit your score.
  • You can switch between activities using the tabs above and revisit them before submitting.

Activity 1: Spot the Bias

Read each scenario about a biased AI system. Select the type of bias, then explain why the training data caused the problem. Each scenario is worth 2 points (1 for the correct bias type, 1 for a strong explanation).

Activity 2: Who's Missing?

Each dataset below has representation gaps. Drag the correct labels from the bank into the drop zone to identify which groups or perspectives are missing. Each dataset is worth 3 points.

Activity 3: Fix the Algorithm

Each biased AI scenario has four possible fixes. Drag them to rank from most effective (#1) to least effective (#4). You earn 3 points for a perfect ranking, 2 for one swap away, 1 for two swaps, and 0 for more. Each scenario is worth 3 points.
